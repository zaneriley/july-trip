Unazuki is the hot-spring town at the entrance to the Kurobe gorge, and the
gateway to the Kurobe Gorge Railway. It's the one part of "Kurobe" you can reach
on a single train: the Hokuriku Shinkansen stops at Kurobe-Unazuki-Onsen station,
though only the Hakutaka service calls there — the faster Kagayaki skips it.
