The hot springs are the point of Atami, strung along the hills above Sagami bay.
Because Atami station is on the Tokaido Shinkansen, it's close enough for an easy
overnight — or even a day trip — without the long haul the mountains ask for.
Note that the fastest Nozomi trains don't stop here; Kodama trains all do.
