Atami is a hot-spring town on the coast of Sagami bay, and one of the closest
onsen towns to Tokyo. It sits right on the Tokaido Shinkansen line, so getting
there is short and simple — which, with a three-year-old along, counts for a lot.
The draw is the easy pace: less time in transit, more time by the water.
