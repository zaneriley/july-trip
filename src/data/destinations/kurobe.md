Kurobe has two faces, and they're a fair distance apart. One is the Tateyama
Kurobe alpine route — a high mountain crossing built from cable cars, tunnel
buses, and a ropeway, with Kurobe Dam at its heart. The other is Unazuki, a
hot-spring town at the mouth of the Kurobe gorge. Both are a real journey from
Tokyo, so the appeal here is the trip itself, not a quick hop.
